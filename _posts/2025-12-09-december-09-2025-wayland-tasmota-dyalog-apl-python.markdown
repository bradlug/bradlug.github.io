<p><strong>Steve</strong> said he had managed to get a Thinkpad on eBay to replace an earlier one for a very reasonable price.</p><p><strong>Brian</strong> asked what would happen to X applications once <a href="https://wayland.freedesktop.org/docs/html/" type="text/html" role="link">Wayland</a> takes over and John said that <a href="https://wayland.freedesktop.org/docs/html/ch05.html" type="text/html" role="link">xwayland</a> allows them to run under Wayland.
